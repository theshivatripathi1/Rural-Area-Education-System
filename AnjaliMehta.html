About

Prof. Anjali Mehra is a dedicated educator in the field of Mathematics, with over 15 years of teaching experience. She specializes in abstract mathematics, calculus, and linear algebra, and focuses on applying mathematical models to solve real-world problems, especially in rural education contexts.

  • M.Sc. in Mathematics – Banaras Hindu University
  • B.Sc. in Mathematics – University of Rajasthan

Experience

  • 15 years of experience in teaching Mathematics at the university level
  • Developed curriculum for high school and university-level Mathematics for rural areas
  • Published research papers on mathematical modeling in rural infrastructure

Courses Handled
